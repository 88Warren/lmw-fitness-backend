5 Nutrition Tips That Actually Make a Difference

Let’s chat about food because training hard without fuelling properly is like putting cheap petrol in a Ferrari.

Here are my top 5 nutrition tips for keeping your energy up, supporting your training and managing weight without the faff.

The 5 Tips

  1. Protein is your best mate
    It helps muscles repair and keeps you fuller for longer. Eggs, chicken, lentils, tofu, mix it up and get some in every meal.
  2. Don’t fear carbs
    They’re your body’s main fuel source. Just go for the good stuff: oats, wholegrain rice, potatoes. They’ll keep your workouts powered and stop those 4pm biscuit raids.
  3. Hydrate like you mean it
    Water first, coffee second. Dehydration sneaks up and makes you feel tired and snacky. Keep a bottle nearby and sip throughout the day.
  4. Colour your plate
    Fruit and veg aren’t just garnish. Aim for a rainbow, the variety means more vitamins, minerals and fibre, all helping you recover and stay healthy.
  5. Balance, not restriction
    Cutting out everything you love never lasts. Enjoy the chocolate, glass of wine or takeaway, just not all at once, every day!
Simple, sustainable nutrition beats any fad diet.

Food is fuel so fuel well!
