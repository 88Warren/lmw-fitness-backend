15 Quick Workouts to Keep Your Training Fun

Have a look at these 15 quick workouts to mix things up and keep your training inspiring

The 15 Workouts

  1. 10-Minute Full-Body Blast
    Squats, press ups, lunges, plank, starjumps – 45s each, 15s rest. Repeat twice. Quick, sweaty, effective.
  2. Deskercise Energy Boost
    Chair squats, desk puress ups, seated twists. Perfect for sneaky energy during work.
  3. HIIT Sprint Circuit
    20s sprint, 40s rest – repeat 8–10 times. Short but brutal.
  4. Core Crusher
    Plank 30s, side plank 30s each side, bicycle crunches 1 min. Repeat twice.
  5. Evening Stretch & Mobility
    Cat-cow, lunges with twist, shoulder rolls. Recover, reset, feel human.
  6. Tabata Toning
    20s work/10s rest: squats, press ups, mountain climbers, star jumps. Repeat 4 rounds.
  7. 5-Minute Cardio Burn
    High knees, squat jumps, butt kicks, starjumps, burpees - 30s repeat. Perfect for a quick sweat.
  8. Glute & Leg Focus
    Glute bridges, squats, walking lunges – 3 sets of 12 each. Tone + strength.
  9. Upper Body Express
    press ups, plank shoulder taps, dorsal raises – 3 rounds of 10–12. Arms and back feel the love.
  10. Morning Wake-Up Circuit
    Starjumps, squats, planks – 30s each. Gets the blood flowing first thing.
  11. Core & Balance
    Plank with shoulder taps, supermans, standing twists – 3 rounds. Strong midsection, better posture.
  12. Quick Tabata for Beginners
    Squats, jog or march on the spot, press ups (on knees), plank holds – 20s on, 10s off. 4 rounds.
  13. Mini HIIT Challenge
    Burpees, mountain climbers, squat jumps – 20s on, 10s rest. Repeat 3 rounds.
  14. Stretch & Strength Combo
    Lunges with overhead reach, cat-cow, downward dog. Balance, mobility, and strength.
  15. End-of-Day Relax & Recover
    Foam roll, seated twists, deep breathing. Soothe muscles and calm the mind.
Quick workouts add up, consistency matters more than duration.
