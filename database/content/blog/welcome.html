A Bit About Me

For those of you who don't know me, I'm a qualified personal trainer and ex-British Army physical training instructor. I've been coaching people for years, trying out all sorts of methods and learning what really works. Let me tell you it's not about endless hours in the gym or extreme diets. It's about time efficient workouts, building healthy habits and finding something that fits into your lifestyle, not the other way round.

I set up LMW Fitness because I genuinely believe fitness should be accessible to everyone, no matter how hectic your schedule. I want to prove that you can make a big difference with short, structured sessions and a positive mindset.

My New 30-Day Fat Loss Programme

One of the things I'm most excited about on my new site is the 30-Day Beginner and advanced Programmes. This is for anyone who's ready to commit but doesn't have hours to spare each day. Here's how it works: you get a workout video from me every single day for 30 days. You can do it at home, in the gym, in the garden, wherever you like. You don't need fancy kit, just a bit of space and the willingness to show up for yourself.

The best bit? You're never on your own. Each video guides you through exactly what to do and I'm right there cheering you on (yes, even through the screen!).
