15 Quick Workouts to Keep Your Training Fun

Hi, it’s Laura! Have a look at these 15 x quick workouts to mix things up and keep your training inspiring:

  1. 10-Minute Full-Body Blast
    Squats, push-ups, lunges, planks – 45s each, 15s rest. Repeat twice. Quick, sweaty, effective.
  2. Deskercise Energy Boost
    Chair squats, desk push-ups, seated twists. Perfect for sneaky energy during work.
  3. HIIT Sprint Circuit
    20s sprint, 40s rest – repeat 8–10 times. Short but brutal.
  4. Core Crusher
    Plank 30s, side plank 30s each side, bicycle crunches 1 min. Repeat twice.
  5. Evening Stretch & Mobility
    Cat-cow, lunges with twist, shoulder rolls. Recover, reset, feel human.
  6. Tabata Toning
    20s work/10s rest: squats, push-ups, mountain climbers, jumping jacks. Repeat 4 rounds.
  7. 5-Minute Cardio Burn
    High knees, butt kicks, jumping jacks, repeat. Perfect for a quick sweat.
  8. Glute & Leg Focus
    Hip thrusts, step-ups, walking lunges – 3 sets of 12 each. Tone + strength.
  9. Upper Body Express
    Push-ups, dumbbell presses, rows – 3 rounds of 10–12. Arms and back feel the love.
  10. Morning Wake-Up Circuit
    Jumping jacks, squats, planks – 30s each. Gets the blood flowing first thing.
  11. Core & Balance
    Plank with shoulder taps, bird dogs, standing twists – 3 rounds. Strong midsection, better posture.
  12. Quick Tabata for Beginners
    Squats, march in place, push-ups, plank holds – 20s on, 10s off. 4 rounds.
  13. Mini HIIT Challenge
    Burpees, mountain climbers, squat jumps – 20s on, 10s rest. Repeat 3 rounds.
  14. Stretch & Strength Combo
    Lunges with overhead reach, cat-cow, downward dog. Balance, mobility, and strength.
  15. End-of-Day Relax & Recover
    Foam roll, seated twists, deep breathing. Soothe muscles and calm the mind.

How did you find them?
